SnapRec - Screen Recorder & Screenshot Tool

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Summary: SnapRec is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All captures remain locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. SnapRec operates entirely on your local device. The extension does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Store any screenshots or recordings on external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Send any data to third parties

2. How the Extension Works

SnapRec provides screen recording and screenshot capabilities that operate entirely within your browser:

  • Screenshots: Captured images are processed locally and saved directly to your device
  • Screen Recording: Videos are recorded and stored locally on your device
  • Annotation Tools: All editing happens within your browser, no data is transmitted

3. Google Drive Integration (Optional)

If you choose to use the optional Google Drive integration:

  • We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • We only request permission to upload files you explicitly choose to save
  • We do not access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive
  • Your Google credentials are never stored by our extension

4. Permissions Explained

The extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • activeTab & tabs: To capture the current page content
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • downloads: To save captures to your device
  • desktopCapture & tabCapture: To enable screen recording functionality
  • scripting: To inject capture scripts into pages

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • You have complete control over your captures
  • No data is transmitted over the internet (except optional Drive uploads)
  • Your captures are as secure as your local device

6. Children's Privacy

SnapRec does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The extension does not collect personal information from any user.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
