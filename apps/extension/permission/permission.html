Allow microphone access

SnapRec needs your microphone to capture your voice during recordings. Click the button below to grant permission.

If the prompt doesn't appear:
1. Look for a microphone icon 🎤 in the address bar.
2. Click it and choose "Always allow".
3. On macOS, also check System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and confirm Google Chrome is enabled. Quit and reopen Chrome if you just changed it.
